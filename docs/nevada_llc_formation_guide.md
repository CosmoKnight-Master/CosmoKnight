## Overview

This guide walks through forming a Nevada LLC for Gambling Excellence. Nevada is a popular choice for privacy and business-friendly statutes. This document is instructional only — you will execute all filings and signatures manually.

**Key Nevada LLC Facts**
- Annual requirements: None initial filing after Articles
- Franchise Tax: $150/year (due by March 31 for annual renewal)
- Privacy: Registered Agent required (can be individual or service)
- Timeline: Same-day approval possible (express filing available)
- Cost: ~$125 filing fee (standard) + Registered Agent fee (varies)

---

## Step 1: Choose a Registered Agent

**Definition:** The RA is the official point of contact for legal documents in Nevada. Can be you or a third-party service.

**Options:**
- **Self (Individual):** You serve as your own RA
  - Pros: Lower cost
  - Cons: Your address becomes public record, requires physical presence for business hours
- **Professional Service:** LegalZoom, Northwest Registered Agent, Agents & Company, etc.
  - Pros: Privacy (their address used), business hours coverage
  - Cons: Annual fee ($50–$300), still requires you for actual service process

**Recommendation for Day 1:** Use a professional RA service if budget allows; if not, register yourself and upgrade later.

**Popular Services (sample list; verify current pricing):**
- Agents & Company: ~$150/year with ongoing support
- Northwest Registered Agent: ~$125/year
- LegalZoom: ~$99/year + platform fees
- Incfile: Often free for first year with LLC package

**Task:** Choose an RA (self or service), get their Nevada address, and confirm their details before filing Articles.

> **Temporary contact info note:** If you used your personal email (e.g. DreamLivingFee@gmail.com) and phone number when completing the SOS form, that's fine — you can amend the registered agent record later once you have dedicated business accounts. Nevada allows updating the agent contact via a simple amendment in eSonorant. Updating keeps state notices from going to your personal inbox/phone.

## Step 2: Prepare Articles of Organization

### Template (Nevada NRS § 86.181)

**File Name:** Articles_of_Organization_GamblingExcellence_LLC.pdf

**Form Contents (fill exactly):**

```
STATE OF NEVADA
SECRETARY OF STATE
DIVISION OF COMMERCIAL RECORDINGS

ARTICLES OF ORGANIZATION
OF LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY

I. The name of the limited liability company is:
   GAMBLING EXCELLENCE LLC

II. The principal business address where books and records are kept is:
   [Your address or a minimal placeholder]
   Example: 1234 Main Street, Las Vegas, Nevada 89101

III. The names, addresses, and signature authority of the managers or 
members controlling the company:

MANAGERS:
Name: [Your Full Legal Name]
Address: [Your Nevada Address or Business Address]
Signature: _________________________ Date: _________

[Add additional managers/members as needed]

IV. The name and Nevada address of the registered agent is:

Name: [RA Name or Service Name]
Address: [RA Nevada Street Address]

Example:
Name: Agents & Company
Address: 1001 N. West B Street, Reno, Nevada 89501

V. The company is to be managed by:
[Select one:]
☑ One or more managers (manager-managed)
☐ Its members (member-managed — less common)

VI. Effective date (Optional):
   [Leave blank for immediate filing, or enter future date]

VII. Other provisions or information:
   [Optional — leave blank unless you have additional provisions]

VIII. Signature

Name and Title of Person Signing: 
[Your Name], Organizer/Member

Signature: _________________________ Date: _________
```

### Key Fields to Fill:
- **Company Name:** Gambling Excellence LLC (exact match to name search)
- **Principal Address:** Your current business/home address in Nevada (if not in Nevada, use a PO Box or virtual office address)
- **Managers:** List all members/owners who will manage the company
- **Registered Agent:** Use the RA details from Step 1
- **Management Structure:** Select "manager-managed" or "member-managed"

### Where to Get the Official Form:
- **NV Secretary of State:** https://www.nvsos.gov/sos/divisions/commercial-recordings/f-llc
- Look for "Articles of Organization Form" (Form LLC-1)

### Filing Options:
1. **In Person:** Nevada Secretary of State Office, Carson City or Las Vegas
2. **By Mail:** Send to NVSOS with check/money order ($125 standard, $75.00 expedited, $300 same-day)
3. **Online (eSonorant):** https://www.nvsos.gov/sos/businesses/e-filing (requires account setup, credit card)

### Recommended Path (Day 1):
- Fill the template PDF
- Upload via eSonorant (fastest)
- Receive confirmation email within hours

## Step 3: Draft Operating Agreement

### Purpose
Defines ownership, management, profit distribution, voting, and responsibilities. Required by best practices (not always legally required, but highly recommended).

### Template (Minimal — Customize as Needed)

**File Name:** Operating_Agreement_GamblingExcellence_LLC.pdf

```
OPERATING AGREEMENT
OF
GAMBLING EXCELLENCE LLC

A Nevada Limited Liability Company

RECITALS

WHEREAS, the undersigned person(s) desire to form a Limited Liability 
Company under the laws of the State of Nevada; and

WHEREAS, the parties intend to enter into this Operating Agreement 
to govern the rights, duties, and obligations of the members and managers.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein, 
the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1: FORMATION AND NAME

1.1 The Limited Liability Company is formed under NRS § 86 et seq. of Nevada.

1.2 The name of the Company is: GAMBLING EXCELLENCE LLC

1.3 The principal place of business is:
    [Your address]

ARTICLE 2: MEMBERS AND CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS

2.1 Members and Capital Contributions:

Name: [Member Name]
Address: [Member Address]
Capital Contribution: [$ amount or description]
Ownership Percentage: [X]%

[Repeat for each member]

2.2 No member is required to make additional capital contributions.

ARTICLE 3: MANAGEMENT

3.1 The Company shall be managed by:
    ☑ Managers (Manager-Managed)
    ☐ Members (Member-Managed)

3.2 Managers:
    The following person(s) shall serve as Manager(s):
    - [Name], Manager
    - [Name], Manager (if applicable)

3.3 Manager Duties:
    Managers shall have the authority to:
    (a) Manage the day-to-day operations of the Company
    (b) Execute and deliver contracts, notes, and instruments
    (c) Employ agents and representatives
    (d) Open bank accounts and authorize signings
    (e) Make capital contributions if required

ARTICLE 4: DISTRIBUTIONS

4.1 Profits and Losses:
    Profits and losses shall be allocated to members pro-rata per ownership 
    percentage.

4.2 Distributions:
    Distributions shall only be made when the Company has positive capital 
    and only with the consent of [specify: all managers / majority vote].

ARTICLE 5: VOTING AND DECISIONS

5.1 Member Voting:
    (a) Routine decisions: No voting required (managers decide)
    (b) Major decisions (dissolution, sale, merger, amendment): Require 
        [majority / unanimous] member vote

5.2 Call for Meetings:
    Any manager may call a meeting of members upon [5 / 10] days' notice.

ARTICLE 6: TRANSFERABILITY OF INTERESTS

6.1 No member may sell, assign, pledge, or transfer an interest without the 
    written consent of [all other members / majority].

6.2 If a member dies or becomes incapacitated, their interest passes to their 
    estate or designated beneficiary.

ARTICLE 7: DISSOLUTION AND WINDING UP

7.1 The Company shall dissolve:
    (a) Upon the death or incapacity of the sole member/manager (unless 
        surviving members agree to continue)
    (b) By unanimous written consent of all members
    (c) Upon a material breach that cannot be cured
    (d) Upon dissolution under Nevada law

7.2 Winding Up:
    Upon dissolution, assets shall be liquidated and distributed in order:
    (1) Debts and liabilities
    (2) Unpaid contributions owed by members
    (3) Remaining capital to members pro-rata

ARTICLE 8: AMENDMENTS

8.1 This Agreement may be amended only by written consent of [all members / 
    majority] and shall be recorded in the Company records.

ARTICLE 9: GOVERNING LAW

9.1 This Agreement shall be governed by Nevada law (NRS § 86).

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Operating Agreement.

MEMBERS/MANAGERS:

Name: _________________________________ Date: __________
Signature: ____________________________

Name: _________________________________ Date: __________
Signature: ____________________________

[Add additional signature blocks for each member/manager]
```

### How to Use This Template:
1. Fill in member names, address, contributions, and percentages
2. Decide: Manager-managed or member-managed (recommend manager-managed for privacy)
3. Specify voting thresholds (majority vs. unanimous)
4. Have all members/managers sign (print, sign, scan, or wet-sign and keep copies)
5. Keep signed copy in your records (do NOT file with state)

### Alternative: Hire a Lawyer
- Cost: $500–$2,000 for customized agreement
- Benefit: Tailored to your specific needs (recommended if you have co-members)

## Step 4: Register with Nevada Secretary of State (if required)

### Annual Filings:
After your LLC is formed, you must:
1. **File Annual List (Form NV-6A):** Due by March 31 each year
   - Lists members, managers, registered agent
   - Filed online via eSonorant
   - Free (included in annual filing fee below)
2. **Pay Franchise Tax:** $150/year, due by March 31
   - Paid to Nevada Secretary of State

## Step 5: Obtain EIN from IRS

After LLC is formed, apply for an EIN (Employer Identification Number).

### Quick Steps:
1. **Online:** https://www.irs.gov/ein
   - Takes ~15 minutes
   - Receive EIN immediately
   - Recommended method
2. **By Phone:** 1-800-829-4933
   - Takes ~20 minutes
   - EIN sent by mail after ~4 weeks
3. **By Mail:** Form SS-4 to IRS (slow, not recommended)

### Information You'll Need:
- Legal business name (Gambling Excellence LLC)
- Principal business address
- Responsible person's SSN (yours)
- Business structure (LLC)
- Reason for applying (newly formed business)

### Save Your EIN:
Once you have your EIN, store it securely. You'll need it for:
- Bank account opening
- Tax filings
- Payroll setup (if hiring)
- Business licenses & permits

## Step 6: Bank Account Setup

After you have:
- Articles of Organization (filed & approved)
- EIN (from IRS)
- Operating Agreement (signed copy)

You can open a business bank account.

### What You'll Need:
1. EIN letter from IRS (or printout from IRS website)
2. Articles of Organization (certified copy from Nevada Secretary of State)
3. Operating Agreement (signed copy)
4. Photo ID
5. Social Security Number (as responsible party)

### Recommended Banks (for small business):
- Chase Business Checking
- Bank of America Business Checking
- Square Banking (fintech-friendly)
- Mercury (startup-focused, remote-friendly)

### Timeline:
- Same-day approval possible
- Debit card within 5–10 business days
- Money transfers available 24–48 hours after opening
